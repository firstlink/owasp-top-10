# TERMINOLOGY RECAP

### Cascade Failure

A sequence of individually reasonable AI decisions combines to produce an unreasonable system outcome.

---

### Cross-Agent Plausibility Validation

Every stage independently verifies whether an upstream recommendation remains plausible before acting on it.

---

### Circuit Breaker

An architectural control that interrupts execution when predefined safety thresholds or abnormal conditions are exceeded.

---

### Blast Radius

The total business impact created when an incorrect decision propagates through interconnected agents without containment.

---

### Notional Cap

A hard exposure limit that prevents an agent from creating orders beyond approved financial thresholds regardless of upstream recommendations.

---

### Digital Twin Testing

An isolated replica of the production environment used to replay historical scenarios and measure cascade effects before deployment, allowing system-wide failures to be discovered without real-world consequences.
